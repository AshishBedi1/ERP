# Statutory public holidays

**Organization:** Finalrope Soft Solutions Pvt. Ltd.  
**Source:** Appointment / employment terms (as communicated in offer documentation).

The company observes **fifteen (15)** paid public holidays per year. Public holidays that fall during an employee’s approved leave do **not** count as part of annual leave.

## List (reference)

1. New Year  
2. Republic Day  
3. Maha Shivratri  
4. Holi  
5. Ram Navami  
6. Mahavir Jayanti  
7. Independence Day  
8. Raksha Bandhan  
9. Gandhi Jayanti  
10. Dussehra  
11. Diwali  
12. Bhai Duj  
13. Guru Nanak Jayanti  
14. Christmas  

**Note:** The source document states 15 holidays but enumerates 14 named days. Confirm the 15th holiday and exact calendar dates with HR each year.

## Related (from employment terms)

- Normal work week: **Monday–Friday**, **9:30–18:30** with a **one (1) hour** break (unless an approved alternative applies).
- Attendance / punctuality and leave policies are documented under `../rules-and-regulations/`.
